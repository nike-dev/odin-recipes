Alunimki (Nepalese Street Food) Recipe

Description

This is authentic snacks from Nepal.It is combination of crispy nimki, spice n sour taste n great snacks for afternoon time.Its easy to make n very much affordable to everyone. Aloo nimki is an incredibly popular street food from the eastern Nepali city of Dharan. It's a crunchy, saucy and flavourful symphony in your mouth. I remember as a kid in Dharan, eagerly anticipating the arrival of the Aloo Nimki vendor.

Ingredients

  • For nimki dough
    • 200gm plain flour
    • 1 tb ghee (butter)
    • 1/2 tsp salt
    • Oil to deep fry
  • 1/2 cup cup water
  • 1/4 dice red onion
  • 3 green chili diced
  • 1 small diced tomato
  • 10 gm soaked chickpeas
  • 1/2 cumin powder
  • 1/4 slice lemon or lime
  • Salt to taste
  • 15 ml Oil
  • 300 ml warm water

Steps

  1. Make a dough adding plain flour,pinch salt n water.rest for few minutes.after that make a round thin roti cut it in zig zag shape n ready to deep fried till golden brown.
  2. For sauce cook besan batter in oil adding little bit chopped garlic,add little bit cumin powder,chilly powder, and salt.stir it well so all ingredients mix well.After that put boiling water till it's thick. N let it be cool down.
  3. After all mis en place ready for alunimki.take a deep round container n add chopped onion,chilly, tomatoes,add spices like cumin,red chilli powder,chat masala,salt n squeeze lemon.after that keep adding sauce, crispy nimki,instant noodles, chopped coriander.mix it well all ingredients n ready to serve in a bowl garnish WD coriander n slice lemon on a side.
